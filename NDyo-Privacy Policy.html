Privacy Policy

Your Data with Trust Security Transparency

Learn how NDyo collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to ensuring your data is handled securely and responsibly at all times.

Terms & Conditions

1. Information We Collect

NDyo collects basic information such as name, email address, phone number, and usage data. This information is collected when users interact with our platform, forms, or services. We may also collect technical data such as device details and usage patterns. This helps us understand how users interact with our system. All data collected is relevant to improving user experience and service quality. We do not collect unnecessary or unrelated personal information.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used to provide, maintain, and improve our services. We use data to personalize user experience and optimize platform performance. It may also be used to communicate updates, support responses, and service-related information. NDyo ensures that user data is used only for legitimate business purposes. We do not use personal information for unauthorized or unrelated activities. All processing is done with a focus on user benefit and service improvement.

3. AI-Data Processing

User inputs such as prompts, voice data, and content may be processed by AI systems. This processing is required to generate outputs and deliver intelligent responses. Data is handled securely during AI processing and is not misused. NDyo may use anonymized data to improve system performance and accuracy. We do not sell or distribute user-generated data to third parties. AI processing is strictly limited to enhancing user experience.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

NDyo does not sell, trade, or rent user personal information to third parties. Data may be shared only when necessary to provide services, such as hosting or payment processing. We may also disclose information if required by law or legal authorities. All third-party services used are selected with strict security considerations. We ensure that user data remains protected during any required sharing. Transparency and trust are maintained in all data-related practices.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access. This includes technical and administrative safeguards. While we strive to protect data, no system can guarantee complete security. Users are encouraged to maintain strong account security practices. NDyo continuously improves its security measures to reduce risks. Protecting user information is a top priority for our platform.

6. User Rights and Updates

Users have the right to access, update, or request deletion of their personal data. Requests can be made through our official communication channels. We respect user control over their data and privacy preferences. NDyo may update this Privacy Policy as services evolve. Any changes will be reflected on this page with updated information. Continued use of the platform indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
